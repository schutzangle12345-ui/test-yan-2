Our Mission

The luxury travel market is fragmented. Booking a private jet, a 100ft yacht, and a secure villa often requires dozen of phone calls, opaque contracts, and fragmented communication across time zones.

We founded BookLux to solve this complexity. By combining sophisticated AI with a deep network of vetted luxury providers, we offer a single, intelligent interface to manage your entire lifestyle.

No more tracking deposits across five vendors. No more opaque pricing. Just seamless execution.

The BookLux Difference

Unlike traditional travel agencies that operate on 9-to-5 schedules, our AI concierge is alive 24/7, providing instant price ranges, itinerary examples, and logistics management for jets, yachts, and villas.

Core Principles

Absolute Transparency

We eliminate the "luxury markup" mystery. Whether it's APA for yachts or empty-leg pricing for jets, we provide clear cost breakdowns so you can decide quickly.

Vetted Excellence

We only partner with providers who meet rigorous safety and service standards. From private chefs to chauffeurs, every service is pre-screened.

Unified Management

One chat. One contract. One point of payment. We handle the multi-vendor complexity so you only have to focus on the destination.

A Global Network

Our intelligence spans the most sought-after regions—from the marinas of the Côte d’Azur and the Amalfi Coast to the winter chalets of the Swiss Alps and the vibrant nightlife of Dubai and Miami.

Whether you need a private chef for an in-villa dining experience or a VIP nightlife concierge for table bookings at exclusive venues, BookLux understands the local etiquette, pricing norms, and safety protocols required.

Risk & Reliability

We manage the fine print. Our system is designed to navigate travel insurance, force majeure clauses for yachts, and weather-related flight shifts. We act as your advocate, ensuring that if plans change, your interests are protected.
